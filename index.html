About Me

MEET CHERIE... I do a greet and meet you and your dog(s). This is free and very important. I watch your dogs at my home. I love to walk the dogs, cuddle with them and spoil them. They should have a good personality and be current on their shots and bordatella, rabies, plus proof of Heart Worm and Flea Tick medications. With proof from the Vets. I passed the background check with five companies. I have an enclosed porch with windows, climate controlled and 2 doggy doors. Fenced. Dogs up to 50 lbs. or if they are easy to take care of. The dogs are allowed on the furniture and free to roam the house of 2000 sq ft. Non Smoking and no drinking.
